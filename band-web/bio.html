    WHY CHOOSE US?

    Since 1998, our rock and roll band has been igniting stages with electrifying performances that resonate with audiences worldwide. With four dedicated members who share a passion for music that knows no bounds, we've crafted a legacy of unforgettable melodies and heart-pounding rhythms.

    Our journey is etched in the annals of rock history, as we've navigated through the ever-changing landscape of the music industry, leaving an indelible mark on every stage we grace. With each chord struck and every lyric sung, we continue to captivate fans, old and new, with our raw energy and unbridled enthusiasm. As we push the boundaries of creativity and musical innovation, our commitment to the timeless spirit of rock and roll remains unwavering. Join us as we embark on the next chapter of our musical odyssey, fueled by the same relentless passion that has defined us since the very beginning.

    OUR MEMBERS

    Grace Davies (Singer)

    Singer Grace Davies (born 16 February 1989) is an English singer-songwriter. She is an independent artist, previously signed to SYCO Music (Sony) before the label's demise in 2010. She is best known for her original song "Roots".

    Brian May (Lead Guitarist)

    The Brian May Brian May, best known as the lead guitarist of the legendary rock band Rock & Roll, is a distinguished musician, songwriter, and astrophysicist. Born on July 19 1997, in England, May's passion for music began at an early age.

    Ginger Baker (Drummer)

    A disciple of the blues and jazz, Baker is considered one of rock's first star drummers. The co-founder of the band. Baker's smooth tempo and infinity for using two bass drums helped create his signature sound.

    Alison Louise (Trumpeter)

    The Alison Louise co-founder (born 7 October 1988) is an English trumpet soloist, arranger, producer, and music educator. Balsom studied at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama, graduating in 2011 with first class honours.

    Ray Morgan (Singer)

    The Ray Morgan was a British singer, who was active from the late 1980s into the 1990s. He scored a chart hit in the UK in 2000. He auditioned on the British music competition series The X Factor.

    Robert Siers (Keyboad)

    Lorem Robert Siers (born March 21, 1987) is an England record producer, musician, and record industry executive. He is among the biggest-selling producers in history, and has produced or had creative involvement in albums that have sold over 130 million units worldwide.
